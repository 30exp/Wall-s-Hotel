WELCOME

Wall's Hotel NYC is the premier long-stay experience. Wall's has become famous for its location, views and clientele.
As a frequent center for influencers, freelancers, professionals and artists we cater to a relaxed atmosphere in which our guests can enjoy the comforts of a high end lifestyle. Wall's brings the luxury of street fashion indoors and onto the walls of every spaces in our New York City high rise building.

Our prime location just a few blocks from the East Village making it easy for our guests to commute to popular hotspots.
Supreme, BAPE STORE® NEW YORK, NOAH NYC, ONLY NY, Round Two, Kith, Gucci, Aimé Leon Dore, Katz's Delicatessen, Momofuku Noodle Bar, Milk Bar Mini: East Village & more.

Our fine dining, sushi bar and Michelin Star awarded café are dedicated to high quality service day & night, year round.
Our house specials, rolls, and blends have been catered by masters from around the world.

Café

Try Our Specialty Blended Teas & Coffee

Enjoy our fine roasted blends cartered to the specifications of those that drink it, out beans have been processed to meet the tastes of those with expensive taste from around the world. Each blend is processed by hand in it's respective country of origin. Our world class trained baristas are dedicated to service and efficiency.

Sushi

Chef On Call All Day & Night

Classic and hand rolls, fusion rolls and special rolls available. We use high quality ahi tuna, salmon, eel, soft-shell crab, white fish, roe (fish eggs), nori seaweed, and avacado. We pair our rolls with specialty soy-sauce, spicy mayo, ponyu, eel sauce & honey miso sauce all made in-house.
